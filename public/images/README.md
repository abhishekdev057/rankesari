# Ran Kesari — Image Manifest

Drop your photos from the `Final Images` folder into the paths below.
Until they exist, the site renders an elegant gold-on-dune SVG placeholder
labelled with the alt text, so nothing breaks.

Recommended export: 16:9 or 4:5 JPG, ~2400px wide, sRGB, ~70% quality.

## Quick tips
- Underexposed twilight / golden hour shots work best with the matte black + gold theme.
- Interior shots: warm tungsten lighting, avoid cool white balance.
- Reserve a hero shot with negative sky space at the top for headline readability.
